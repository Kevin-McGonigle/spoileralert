SpoilerAlert Spoiler Classification Survey

Hi there! We are David Early and Kevin McGonigle, two third year students in the BSc in Computer Applications and Software Engineering degree in Dublin City University (DCU). As part of our third year project, under the supervision of Dr. Suzanne Little, we are creating a Google Chrome extension called "SpoilerAlert" that will detect and hide any spoilers for the popular HBO television series, Game of Thrones. This project will serve as a proof of concept for a much more general spoiler blocker with potential to block spoilers for any TV show or movie.

One of the primary obstacles that we must overcome is the ambiguity and subjectivity in relation to what a spoiler actually is. The results of this survey will allow us to resolve these issues and serve as the basis for how our application will adjudge something as a spoiler or not.

No personal data will be collected as part of this survey and you will have the option of withdrawing from the survey at any time. However, information provided cannot always be guaranteed by researchers and can only be protected within the limitations of the law - i.e., it is possible for data to be subject to subpoena, freedom of information claim or mandated reporting by some professions. Should you decide to participate in this survey, you will be given a series of sentences relating to Game of Thrones and you will be asked to specify whether the given text is a spoiler or not. There are no correct answers to these questions, this survey is simply asking for your opinion. If you are interested in finding out what happens with the project, you may get in touch with us at david.early2@mail.dcu.ie and kevin.mcgonigle2@mail.dcu.ie or by contacting the DCU School of Computing.
